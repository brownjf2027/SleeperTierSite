Track player tiers live in your Sleeper draft

No login or account required!

While fantasy football projections can provide valuable insights and guidance for fantasy team managers, they should be used as just one tool among many.

If you like to categorize players into Tiers for drafts, this tool is designed for you. You'll be able to download a CSV template to input Tier values to as many players as you'd like and upload back to the site for live tracking.
